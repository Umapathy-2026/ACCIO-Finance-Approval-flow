ACCIO is a Finance Accounts Receivable (AR) ticketing and approval web application built with Flask. It enables employees (requesters) to submit finance-related requests (e.g., invoice corrections, VAT issues, rebates), attach supporting documents, and route them to assigned approvers for review. Approvers process tickets through a clear workflow with approval, rejection, and clarification loops, while admins manage users, forms, and audit logs. The business purpose is to centralize AR process control, reduce email/Excel sprawl, provide auditable approval trails, and streamline fulfilment through standardized forms and notifications.
